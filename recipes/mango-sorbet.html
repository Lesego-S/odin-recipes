Mango Sorbet Recipe

Description

This dreamy mango sorbet is a great dessert to make with fresh ripe mangos to cool off on hot summer days. Hope you enjoy this as much as my family does!

Ingredients

  • 4 mangos - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 cup simple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Place cubed mango in a food processor, and puree. Pour in simple syrup and lime juice, and puree until smooth.
  2. Place in an ice cream maker. Freeze thoroughly.
